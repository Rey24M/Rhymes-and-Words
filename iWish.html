I Wish

I wish I could reach the other horizon To leave the shore and go on discovering
Find a place that is mine to belong
Where I have everything for which I long
A world animated out of my thoughts
That's what I want

I wish I could touch the end of the sky
Just for the sake of getting high
I want to purify the air of affection
And come back with a sense of satisfaction

I wish I could colour someone's black and white dream
To weave a dream that never finished
Make them realise that nothing ever ends
That nothing ever stays

I wish I could flow in sync with the wind
Go away everywhere in a single ride
Never stay put for something
That was never mine to begin with

Just never ever stop, I wish

-By Mish & Ridz
