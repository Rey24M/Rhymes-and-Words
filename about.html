About

Rhymes and Words

Rhymes and words is a project of two curious 9th graders. It is a hub for creative writing.

Creative writing, as defined by wikipedia is any writing that goes beyond the bounds of normal, professional, journalistic, academic and technical form of literature.

We, at Rhymes and Words believe that writing is one of the best ways of storytelling. It is a vast and diverse form to express oneself in the most subtle yet impactful manner.

It is a hobby website which allows me, who writes essays and my best friend, who writes poems, show our works to all. It can be for a good read or an inspiration.

People can also email us to suggest new topics or contribute with their own works. It is a happy place of exchange to promote creative writing.
