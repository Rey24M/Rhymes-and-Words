A World Without Me and You

After 20 more years of exhausting life, when we are all tired
Of excess fame or of not being admired
Take a walk down the memory lane
To these childhood days and it’s fun insane

When you find no reason to take a walk
Or with loved ones you find no reason to talk
Think about when with family and friends we used to hang out
And never shut our babbling mouth

When frustrated about profits of life
And forget to smile in it’s stride
Take a walk down the memory lane
To when there was no stress, no worry, no loss, no gain

When being drained into a path of wrong
Which will be regretted lifelong
Do remember this childhood time
When lying to parents was also a crime

When stressed with life and it’s worry
When life’s in a rush and in a hurry
Don’t forget to get wet in the rain
While taking a walk down the memory lane

-By Mridunja Raman
